    Arcade Machine

    Arcade Machine Maintenance

    Arcade machines are robust by nature however with time and heavy use by grown adults parts can start to malfunction especially the joystick and buttons which are the most frequent parts to deteriorate over time.

    Should any internal wiring become loose overtime (or via transportation) we are able to restore connectivity or replace any malfunctioning wires.

    We also stock all other parts from the machine including screen, sound card and speakers and the games system.

    Request a quote to restore your arcade machine to brand new to enjoy the ultimate gaming experience again.

    Please note we offer this service only for arcade machines provided by the London Games Room.

    Table Cricket

    Table Cricket Maintenance

    The next generation table top cricket machine by Throwmotion is a complex machine in essence due to its automatic scoring system which uses a large array of sensors and electronics.

    Over time and with heavy use and transportation (if used for promotional events) parts can need replacing.

    The two most common incidents are the loss of balls and the wicket breaking off due to misuse.

    We store balls and wickets can easily be replaced by the user, however if the wicket breaks off cleanly and cannot be removed by hand it is necessary to dismantle the batting control box to remove the broken part and replace it.

    We also store all the other parts of the machine should any part fail and need replacement.

    London Games Room is the official service and maintenance agent of Throwmotion machines in the United Kingdom.
