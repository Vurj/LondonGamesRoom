    Customisation

    Table Football

    Customise your table

    Personalising your Babyfoot for either home, club or work use is as simple as completing five simple steps. And with a minimum order of one, theres nothing to stop you from making it your own.

    Choose your finish

    All cabinets are handmade and can be made in a choice of finishes that include natural beech, walnut, cherry, white leaded, painted in your choice of colour.

    Decide on the legs

    The solid timber legs usually come in black but why not throw some colour on them? Of course you could go au naturel or even clear perspex. Lots of options.

    Change the rails

    With a dozen colours to choose from, the rails are easily customised to match your company, team or personal preferences.

    Put it in writing

    Our in-house design team are on hand to make sure that anything that can be done, is. Well use your artwork or create new designs for you. Your choice.

    Make your mark

    Get creative. Multi-layered screen printing, permanent adhesive stickers or even temporary vinyl letters can all be applied at the factory.

    Pick the teams

    And don't forget the team strips. One or two bespoke painted teams, female players even special unique casts are all possible. We've even done Barbie dolls!

    Arcade Machines

    Customise your arcade

    Personalising your arcade for either home, club or work use is as simple as completing five simple steps. And with a minimum order of one, theres nothing to stop you from making it your own.

    Choose your Marquee

    A marquee is a sign above the monitor displaying a particular game, design or logo you wish to have. Back lighting option is also available.

    Choose the T-Molding

    Choose your T-Molding (trim) in any primary colour to suit your overall design. Also available in metallic finish such as gold, silver and bronze.

    Customise the cabinet

    All cabinets are handmade and can be in a choice of finishes that include all primary colours in matt or gloss.

    Add Side Vinyl Graphics

    Add side vinyl graphics to fully customise the look of your cabinet. Graphics can be provided or we can help you create your own. There is no limit to the amount of colours or type of design for your customised vinyl graphics.

    Add Front Vinyl Graphics

    Add front vinyl graphics to fully customise the look of your cabinet. Graphics can be provided or we can help you create your own. There is no limit to the amount of colours or type of design for your customised vinyl graphics.
