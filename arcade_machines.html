    Retro Prime Arcade Machine

    Brand new replica of classic arcade machines with multi game system to play all the best retro games since the birth of video gaming.


    The arcade cabinets are built for one or two players to play simultaneously depending on the chosen game. Components used to build the cabinet and electronics are from a commercial grade suppliers making the machine robust and reliable over time. The arcade is powered using Raspberry Pi 3 motherboard and an emulation operating system. Games selection can easily can updated using a micro memory card.

    - Built in 19 inch monitor covered by Perspex
    - High spec black body paint with extra coat of lacquer
    - Commercial grade joysticks and buttons
    - Built in high quality speakers above screen
    - Sound Amplifier controls at rear for ease of use
    - Power switch at rear for ease of use

    Please note you are being supplied with a high quality gaming cabinet. This cabinet has been built with the ability to play various arcade games in one unit. All games are included free of charge and are not considered part of the sale price.
